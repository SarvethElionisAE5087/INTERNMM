# Product Sales Analysis

## Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a product sales dataset to identify top-performing products, revenue-generating categories, city-wise sales performance, and monthly sales trends.

## Objectives

* Clean and preprocess the dataset
* Identify top-selling products
* Analyze category-wise revenue distribution
* Perform city-wise sales analysis
* Visualize monthly sales trends
* Study the relationship between price and quantity sold
* Generate business recommendations

 Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Google Colab

 Dataset Features

* Product ID
* Product Name
* Category
* Price (USD)
* Quantity Sold
* Total Sales (USD)
* Order Date
* Customer City

Visualizations

1. Top 5 Products by Revenue (Bar Chart)
2. Category Revenue Share (Pie Chart)
3. Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
4. Price vs Quantity Sold (Scatter Plot)
5. Correlation Heatmap

Key Findings

* Sports category generated the highest revenue.
* Peshawar was the top revenue-generating city.
* Tennis Ball was the highest-selling product.
* Price and Quantity Sold showed almost no correlation (-0.03).

Business Recommendations

* Focus inventory on top-performing products.
* Run targeted campaigns in high-revenue cities.
* Use monthly sales trends for inventory planning.
